Flagship Division

Taj Foods

Connecting the world's best harvests with the Middle East's most demanding tables.

Our Story

Quality Food, Delivered Fresh

Taj Foods is the foundation upon which the Taj Middleeast Group was built. For over 15 years, we have been a trusted partner for businesses and consumers alike, providing a consistent supply of premium food products sourced from the finest producers globally.

Our operation is dual-faceted, combining a massive B2B wholesale distribution network with our community-centric Taj MiniStore retail brand. This unique model allows us to maintain strict quality control from the source all the way to the consumer's basket.

Wholesale Reach

A primary supplier to leading hotels, restaurants, and catering companies across the GCC with over 500 SKUs.

Retail Excellence

Our Taj MiniStores offer a curated grocery experience, bringing quality and convenience to local neighbourhoods.

Cold Chain Logistics

State-of-the-art temperature-controlled warehousing and transport ensuring maximum freshness and safety.

Global Sourcing

Direct partnerships with farmers and producers worldwide to ensure the best quality at the most competitive prices.

Divisional Specializations

  • Staple Commodities: Bulk supply of grains, pulses, sugar, and edible oils for institutional buyers.
  • Fresh Produce: Seasonal fruits and vegetables sourced daily and distributed through our rapid logistics network.
  • Specialty Groceries: A wide range of spices, condiments, and gourmet products for diverse culinary needs.
  • Private Labeling: Custom food sourcing and packaging solutions for large-scale retail partners.

Partner with Taj Foods

Whether you are a retail distributor or a professional kitchen, we have the products and the reliability you need to grow.
