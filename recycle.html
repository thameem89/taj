Taj Recycle

Transforming waste into resources through innovative industrial solutions.

Overview

Leading the Circular Economy

Taj Recycle is a premier division of the Taj Middleeast Group, recognized as one of the largest industrial scrap processors and waste management providers in the region. We are committed to supporting a sustainable future by transforming industrial waste into valuable resources.

Aligned with regional sustainability goals, our operations are designed to maximize resource recovery while minimizing environmental impact. We process over 1 million tons of waste annually across our state-of-the-art facilities.

Dismantling & Demolition

Engineering-driven services for industrial sites, factories, and civil structures, from planning to site clearance.

Scrap Processing

Expert collection and sorting of ferrous and non-ferrous metals, plastics, and non-metallic scrap.

Circular Solutions

End-to-end waste management programs that convert industrial byproducts into raw materials.

Integrated Logistics

A dedicated fleet and logistics network ensuring efficient transportation of materials across the GCC.

Our Core Capabilities

  • Industrial Demolition: Safe and efficient decommissioning of large-scale industrial plants.
  • Metal Recycling: High-precision processing of steel, copper, aluminum, and brass.
  • Sustainable Waste Management: Customized programs for oil & gas, petrochemical, and manufacturing sectors.
  • Logistics Excellence: Reliable collection and transportation powered by our proprietary network.
