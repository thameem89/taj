Circular Economy

1 Million Tons Annually.

Taj Recycle is a critical infrastructure partner for industrial giants across the GCC. We process over one million tons of industrial waste annually, providing end-to-end solutions for the management, sorting, and recovery of valuable resources.

Our engineering-led approach ensures that every project — from factory demolition to metal recovery — is handled with the highest safety standards and environmental responsibility.
