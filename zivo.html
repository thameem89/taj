Fine Fragrances

Zivo Perfumes

Unveiling the art of scent through exquisite craftsmanship and timeless elegance.

Our Essence

Crafting Unforgettable Scents

Zivo Perfumes is the pinnacle of luxury within the Taj Middleeast Group. We specialize in the creation of sophisticated fragrances that blend the rich traditions of Middle Eastern perfumery with contemporary global trends. Our mission is to provide scents that are not just smelled, but experienced.

Every bottle of Zivo is a testament to our commitment to quality, featuring premium ingredients sourced from the world's most renowned regions and blended by master perfumers to create unique olfactory journeys.

Signature Blends

Exclusive fragrances crafted using rare ingredients to create a unique and lasting impression.

Luxury Oud

A dedicated collection celebrating the "Black Gold" of perfumery, available in pure and blended forms.

Bespoke Services

Personalized fragrance consultations and custom blending for individuals and corporate gifting.

Artisan Bottles

Exquisite packaging and bottle designs that reflect the luxury and sophistication of the scent within.

Olfactory Collections

  • The Heritage Collection: Rich, deep, and traditional scents rooted in Oriental history.
  • Modern Muse: Fresh, floral, and contemporary fragrances for the modern individual.
  • Imperial Oud: A selection of the finest aged oud oils and blends for the true connoisseur.
  • Discovery Sets: Curated samplers allowing you to explore the diverse range of Zivo's masterpieces.
